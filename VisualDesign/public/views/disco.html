Danceability

Depicted above is the danceability of chart entries from each year. Longitude represents a specific year, while altitude encodes chart position. Therefore, each tile provides information on a specific song within the Billboad Hot 100 Year-End Charts between 1959 and 2024.
Each tile contains information about a specific song. Danceability is measured in values ranging from 0 to 1 and indicated by the brightness of the respective tile. In some cases, data might be missing. The respective fields are then colored green.

Bias Information:
Since not all tiles are of similar size, keep in mind that tilesize does not contain or encode any extra information. Hence, if a specific area seems bright but of small of size, remember that many songs might be encoded within that area of the ball. This effect occurs toward the top and bottom (relative to the starting point of the visualization)!
