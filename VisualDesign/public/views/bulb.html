Energy

Shown above is the development of the average energy of chart-listed songs in a specific positional interval.
Intervals contain chart positions 1-10 & 11-20. This visualization displays the course of average energy within the top 20% positions of the Billboard Hot 100 Year-End Charts. The more mean energy is found within the chart-positional interval represented, the brighter a light bulb will shine. Since it is an animated visual, the energy level's development across time becomes apparant when starting the animation by clicking the button underneath page navigation. Next to the start/pause button are 2 sliders, the left one indicating the current year (also depicted beneath the animated light bulbs), while the right controls the animation's speed. Once finished, the animation will start again until it is paused using the button pressed before.
