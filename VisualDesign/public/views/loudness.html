Loudness

This graph represents the mean loudness of a chart-listed song evolving over time. For this, the average loudness of a single song if has again been averaged over all chart entries within each year. Each vertical line represents a year, while it's amplitude (distance to baseline, in this visual symmetric) encodes the average loudness of a song in the respective year. Hovering over a vertical bar opens a tooltip display the year and it's average song-loudness. By clicking on a bar, another visualization appears, providing more detailed information about the loudness of individuals songs and the distribution of loudness relative to chart position.
